Lesson 1: The Parts of a Computer

A computer is made of a few main parts that work together.

  • The monitor - the screen you look at.
  • The keyboard - the keys you type with.
  • The mouse - the device you use to point and click.
  • The tower or laptop body - where the computer does its thinking.

Lesson 2: Using the Mouse

Follow these steps to get comfortable with the mouse.

  1. Rest your hand on the mouse and move it slowly. Watch the arrow on the screen move with it.
  2. Press the left button once. This is a "click."
  3. Press the left button twice quickly. This is a "double-click."
